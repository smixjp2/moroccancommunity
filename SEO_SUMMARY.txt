🚀 NEXT STEPS (4 simple steps)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1️⃣  CONFIGURE ENVIRONMENT
    • Copy .env.example to .env.local
    • Set: NEXT_PUBLIC_BASE_URL=https://themoroccancommuntiy.com
    • Set: GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION=your_google_code
    ⏱️ Time: 5 minutes

2️⃣  CREATE OG IMAGES
    • Design 8 images @ 1200x630px
    • Save to /public folder:
      - og-image.png (default)
      - og-image-home.png
      - og-image-about.png
      - og-image-articles.png
      - og-image-tools.png
      - og-image-contact.png
      - og-image-resources.png
      - og-image-news.png
    • Use: Figma, Canva, or Adobe Express
    ⏱️ Time: 15-30 minutes

3️⃣  BUILD & TEST
    • npm run build
    • npm start
    • Visit http://localhost:9002/sitemap.xml ✅
    • Visit http://localhost:9002/robots.xml ✅
    ⏱️ Time: 10 minutes

4️⃣  DEPLOY & SUBMIT
    • Deploy to production
    • Add domain to Google Search Console
    • Submit /sitemap.xml
    • Monitor indexation
    ⏱️ Time: 10 minutes
